Welcome to the first post on the SoftwareChip blog. This is where I'll share thoughts on building software, design philosophy, and the journey of creating products as a solo developer.

I started SoftwareChip with a simple belief: software doesn't have to be bloated to be powerful. It doesn't have to track you, analyze your behavior, or pump ads into your face. It can be clean, minimal, and respectful.

I will try to post more often and release more projects in the future.
